Why do businesses
should choose Globalpay?

Globalpay is the payment processor of choice for some of the fastest-growing businesses in Bangladesh. Here're 12 reasons why.

01

Start accepting payments instantly

Globalpay is the fastest, simplest way to start accepting online payments in Nigeria. From signup to receiving real payments can take under 15 minutes. Seriously.

02

Simple, transparent pricing

Zero integration fee. Zero maintenance fee.

Globalpay only charges a tiny fee per successful transaction, which means we literally only make money when you do, and we work hard to ensure every transaction succeeds.

See our pricing

03

Delight customers with a seamless payments experience

Give your customers the gift of modern, frictionless, painless payments. Integrate Globalpay once and let your customers pay you however they want.

  • Card
  • Bank Account
  • Bank Transfer
  • USSD
  • Visa QR
  • Mobile Money
  • POS (Coming soon)

04

Enjoy phenomenal transaction success rates

We automatically route payments through the most optimal channels, ensuring the highest transaction success rates in the market.

Find out how we achieve high success rates

05

Protect yourself and your customers with advanced fraud detection

Globalpay’s combination of automated and manual fraud systems protect you from fraudulent transactions and associated chargeback claims.

  • PCI-DSS-certified systems
  • Automated fraud monitoring
  • When a fraud attempt is discovered with any Globalpay merchant, ALL Globalpay merchants become protected from further attempts

06

Build custom payments experiences with well-documented APIs

Developers love our thorough, well-documented APIs that let you to build everything from simple weekend projects, to complex financial products serving hundreds of thousands of customers. If you can imagine it, you can build it with Globalpay.

  • Collect one-time and recurring payments from your app or website
  • Make instant transfers
  • Retrieve all your transaction and customer data
  • Verify the identity of customers
Globalpay API Quickstart

07

Understand exactly how your business is performing

Globalpay provides detailed reporting, alerts, and exports, so you and your team are always on top of business.

  • Super easy reconciliation
  • Detailed reports on successful, abandoned, and failed transactions
  • Granular user permissions lets you control the info each member of your team has access to
  • Automated chargeback alerts
  • Searchable Customer Directory
  • Analyze customer purchase patterns with Customer Insights

08

Get your money within 24 hours

Receive your money automatically in your bank account within 24 hours after payment is made. We settle all merchants before 10 AM, every morning.

09

Get paid by customers from anywhere in the world

The internet has no borders, and neither should your business. With Globalpay, you can accept payments from international customers, wherever they are in the world.

You can accept payments from internationally-issued cards, display prices in USD, and also get settled in USD.

We also make available localized payment options - such as mobile money - that are popular in certain regions of the world.

10

Get attentive, empathetic help 24/7

Our Customer Success team is available around the clock to support you, and they’re empowered to resolve issues as quickly as possible.

11

A modern, beautiful payments experience

Give customers confidence in your business with a modern, gorgeous checkout form that elevates your brand.

  • No redirects - yup, keep your customers on your own page
  • Automatic error resolution - in the rare occasion where a transaction fails, the Globalpay Checkout Form smartly suggests other payment options

12

A team that's obsessed about your growth

Above all, over 60,000 businesses love Globalpay because we’re a team that’s obsessed with your company’s growth. The mission of Globalpay is to help businesses in Africa be profitable, envied, and loved, and every thing we do - from the features we build to how we hire - is done with a single goal: to guarantee your business’ success.
